Mediterranean Mischief

Here is the story of Tiberius and his party as they travel across the Mediterranean 

Beginnings

Our Party met at the Athenian Games in the year 508 BCE where we each partook in the some of athletic competitions.

However, in their original purpose for coming to athens was not the games.

They were in fact hired for an assassination.

The night after the games had concluded, they put their plan into motion.

The Assassination

As the closing ceremony for the games took place on the streets of athens, our party lied in wait within the crowd, making their way closer to the speaking platform having prepared the day before 

The each member took their place ready to strike.
